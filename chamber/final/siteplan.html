Site Name

IT professionals of Utah County

This name represents a community of IT professionals based in Utah County

Optional Domain Availability: itutc.org

Site Purpose

The IT professionals of Utah County website serves as a central hub for regional IT professionals. It offers:

  • Advice on doing IT in the area
  • Advice on businesses to try to get jobs at
  • General IT information
  • Event calendars for group workshops.

Scenarios

Potential questions from site visitors:

  • Do i fit in to this community?
  • Where can I find contact information for the community leaders

Color Scheme

This is the website color scheme

  • Primary Color: SeaGreen (#2E8B57) – Used for headers, navigation bars, and key highlights.
  • Secondary Color: Gold (#FFD700) – Applied to subheadings, icons, and background accents.
  • Accent Color: OrangeRed (#FF4500) – Utilized for call-to-action buttons and important alerts.

Background Color: WhiteSmoke (#F5F5F5) – Provides a clean and neutral backdrop for content.

Text Color: Dark Gray (#333333) – Ensures readability against the light background.
